Site Name

Puebla Chamber of Commerce. This name represents the city in which the chamber was founded.

Site Purpose

The Puebla Chamber of Commerce promotes the free trade, transparency and the best practices within and outside the business sector, helping its members to improve and grove their businesses, while maintaining strong business relationships between Mexico and the US.

Scenarios

Questions asked by a visitor:

  • What advises would the Chamber give to a new entrepeneur in order to grow his business?
  • What are some services that the Chamber provides to its members?
  • How can I join the Chamber and what are the benefits of doing it?
